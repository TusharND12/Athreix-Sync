## Core Features (use 3–4 in the video)

1. **Real-time WebRTC Mesh** — Devices connect instantly; files transfer peer-to-peer (no cloud)
2. **Instant Peer Discovery** — No QR codes, no pairing
3. **Universal Clipboard** — Copy on phone, paste on desktop
4. **Burn After Reading** — Ephemeral files vanish after viewing
5. **Passkey-Secured Transfers** — Zero-knowledge protection
6. **AI Intent Commands** — e.g. "Send report.pdf to Node_4a2b"
7. **Unlimited File Sizes** — Direct device-to-device, no cloud caps

---

## Target Audience

- Creators, developers, and teams sharing large files fast
- Privacy-focused users who don't want cloud uploads
- Multi-device users (phone ↔ laptop ↔ tablet)
